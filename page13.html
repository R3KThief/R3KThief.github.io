**Universal Unit/Vehicle/Char Traits**

Apoc Cannon: For penetration, roll 2D10 and add the result, -1D6 extra structure points.
Mortal Wound: Targets that have a maximum of more than one wound that are wounded by a weapon with “Mortal Wound” trait are automatically killed.
Fusion: If target is at half range or less, roll two dice for armour penetration for each hit and take the highest roll.
Melta: Reroll failed armour penetration rolls vs vehicles.
Power Weapon: Ignores armour saves (inv saves allowed).
Djin: Weilder makes 2 additional attacks, but if doubles are rolled, the weapon hits the bearer instead.
Incorporeal: 6+ inv save vs all energy and psychic ranged/CC hits; 4+ inv save VS all other ranged/CC hits.
Abductor Claw: For each wound scored in CC, bearer rolls a 1D6, on a 5+, bearer heals 1 wound (cannot exceed base maximum).
Charger: +1 STR to CC attacks the turn a unit charges into CC.
Tail Whip: Bearer uses his/her vehicle to make one special CC attack per turn in lieu of all other attacks (R-b2b S-+1 AP-4 SP-Always hits, Rending)
Dias: Passengers on vehicle may make CC attacks during CC phase, but may not shoot ranged weapons.
Holo Field: When enemy shoots bearer, roll two dice for each shot and take the lowest.
Rending Claws: Vs vehicles in CC, roll to hit, then roll for armour pen, any 6’s automatically count as glancing hits.
Loyalty: Model can confer one wound to a friendly unit within 6”, once per CC round.
Acrobatic: Unit charges up to 7” instead of 6”, 5+ invulnerable save vs all CC attacks.
Burning Attacks: Model/Unit’s CC attacks gain the Power Weapon ability
Grot (as)Ammo: +1D3 Str, +1 to glancing, penetration, haywire results; remove one Grot from party.
Re-Zzap!: If a 1 is rolled on Penetration/Wounding roll, weapon is disabled for remainder of battle, or until repaired.
Shield (vehicle): this value, based on a 1D10 roll is a save that can be used once per turn, per vehicle in the shooting phase only, resolved after to-hit roll (bearer chooses if/when to use shields).
Structure Killer: Vehicles/Structures hit by this weapon suffer -2 Structure points instead of -1 per hit.
Vortex: Inflicted vehicles are moved 1D6” +scatter dice and receive a glancing hit; inflicted units are pinned.
Infiltrate: Unit may deploy before all other units, and up to 18” of enemy’s deployment zone. If both players have Infiltrators, roll to see who deploys first then take turns.
Stealth: Unit deploys as Infiltrators; use random numbered place markers and write down which marker is the actual unit (unit is not engaged until controlling player decides or enemy player guesses correct marker during shooting/CC phase).
Relocate: Instead of assaulting, unit may make a free 1D6 move in any direction.
Repair Vehicle: Refers to Grots and Servitors; once per turn, one unit on board or in B2B contact with vehicle is sacrificed to repair one Weapon Destroyed, Immobilized, 1 Structure Point, or Shield functionality. Vehicle cannot move/fire destroyed weapon being repaired until the following turn.
